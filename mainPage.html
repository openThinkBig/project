what we offer

We divided the functions of our records into four categories. This website provides the following features.

Memo

Leave your memories on a random set of colorful notes!
You can check it at any time and delete it.

Check List

Check out what you need to do.
A well-organized list will help you not forget your plans!

Travel Note

Record your travel experiences.
The map makes it more convenient to manage, and you can search for the areas you went to.

Movie/Book

Enjoy a variety of movies and books to suit your taste and leave them here.
You can come back as much as you want and feel the emotions of that time vividly.
